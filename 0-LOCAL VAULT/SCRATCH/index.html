    I love productivity and getting things done. It just feels so good to start, progress, and finish a task. What makes productivity satisfying to me is that when I work, I believe that I am making progress towards the completion of my calling.

    My love for productivity came from an understanding of myself. God has revealed my identity and my purpose, which He also continues to develop.

    I love games and constantly have to put down the controller. God showed me why I love games. I play a lot of character development games like RPGs and looting games. I like progression, building stats, and obtaining items to make my character or teams strong. God showed me that I’m meant to do that in the real world with myself and others. So, in effect, I love personal development, teaching, and Project Management(Just to name a few...). I love to see people and things grow from one stage to another. So, my constant focus is seeking how I can grow myself and others up for the building of God’s Kingdom.

    How to apply the Word of God(The Bible) into your everyday life. How to live a truly fulfilled life. Adding small bits of fulfillment/satisfaction.

    The role of a disciple encompasses all areas of a person’s life. Being a disciple for Christ is a lifestyle.
